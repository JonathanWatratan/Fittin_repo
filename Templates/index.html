Fittin is an AI-based health awareness assistant designed to help students recognize early signs of potential physical health issues. By providing simple observations and health guidance, Fittin encourages users to stay mindful of their body and maintain a healthier lifestyle.

What We Offer

Built for your health, by design

Fittin combines modern AI with a thoughtful user experience to help you understand your symptoms and take the right next steps.

Symptom Analysis

Describe your symptoms and get structured, educational insights about possible causes — never a diagnosis.

AI Conversation

Continue a natural conversation with Fittin AI to explore your health questions in a safe, supportive space.

Emergency Detection

Our system flags critical symptoms immediately and advises you to seek urgent medical help right away.

Health Education

Access easy-to-understand health information that promotes awareness and encourages professional consultation.

Private & Secure

Your health information stays private. No personal data is stored or shared without your knowledge.

Mobile Friendly

Access Fittin from any device, anytime. Fully responsive and optimized for phones, tablets, and desktops.

The Process

How Fittin works

Three simple steps to better health awareness. No medical background required.

1

Fill in your health details

Enter your basic info, primary symptom, pain level, and any additional notes in our guided form.

2

AI analyses your input

Fittin's AI processes your data and generates a structured educational response about possible causes and self-care.

3

Continue the conversation

Ask follow-up questions in the chat interface and get further guidance — always with a reminder to see a professional.
